Cheesekut

This Cheesekut is incredibly easy to make and nice. No one can deny its simple decadence. It is confirmed that you'll search for a second plate once you had one. Once is never enough. One simple amazeballs recipe that can keep your guests at awe. Let's try it out!

Ingredients:

  • HupSeng Crackers - 125g (or other crackers brands)
  • Cream cheese - 150g (any brands)
  • Condensed Milk - 130g
  • Milk - 50g
  • Nestum - 30g

Step:

  1. In a bowl, add in cream cheese and condensed milk together.
  2. Mix it well until smooth and well combined.
  3. Prepare a pan, or any tupperware, then take a small amount of cheese mixture, and spread it over.
  4. Prepare a bowl of milk, dip HupSeng cracker in it just to soften the cracker.
  5. Arrange the cracker over the pan until all area is covered.
  6. Then spread some cheese mixture on top evenly, and pour a small amount of nestum on it evenly.
  7. Repeat this step until the cheese mixture is finished.
  8. Make sure the last layer is a cheese mixture and topped with some nestum.
  9. Fridge it for at least 1 hour before consuming.

Voila! You have your super yummy and easy to make cheesekut, or in its famous term, Ciskut.



Batik Cake

One of Malaysia's favourite local cakes is the Batik Cake. We simply can't resist the fudgy chocolate, made most often with Milo. I give you a Batik Cake recipe with easy steps to cook this awesome cake. The best part about this recipe - no baking! You must try it!



Ingredients:

  • 1 small can of condensed milk
  • 1 3/4 cups chocolate milk powder / Milo powder
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 250 - 300 grams Marie Biscuits

Step:

  1. Crush the biscuits into small pieces and set them aside.
  2. Prepare a cake pan (any shape of your choosing) and grease well with cooking spray or butter.
  3. Gently melt butter on medium heat in a pot.
  4. Mix in condensed milk and chocolate/Milo powder and stir it well.
  5. Add in eggs and stir continuously on medium to medium-high heat till it thickens.
  6. Once it has thickened, add in vanilla extract and stir to mix it all well.
  7. Put the crushed biscuits into the pan or pot and stir gently. Pour into the cake pan and flatten the top.
  8. Spread a layer of fudge onto the cake pan and stack it with the biscuits if you don't want the biscuits to be crushed. Repeat until you've used up all of your fudge.
  9. Leave the cake to cool for an hour then cover and refrigerate overnight.

Mhmm, can you smell it already? Let the moment you bite into your own Batik Cake be a memory that lasts forever.



Banana Cake

A Banana Cake is a cake prepared using banana as a primary ingredient and typical cake ingredients. It can be prepared in various manners, including as a layer cake, as muffins and as cupcakes. I'll give you this recipes.



Ingredients:

  • 300g banana
  • 3 eggs
  • 35g oil
  • 125g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch baking soda
  • 30g sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Step:

  1. Smash banana into lumpy paste.
  2. Add eggs and oil and stir until well mixed.
  3. Sift the dry ingredients and add it into the banana batter little by little.
  4. Mix until fully incorporated.
  5. Transfer it into a baking mold and bake it in 180C' oven for 35 minutes

Now you get a super yummy Banana Cake!
